1 Subdomains typically refer to the portion of a domain preceding the top two levels. For example, in
2 the domain api.contoso.com, api is a subdomain of the contoso.com domain (which itself is
3 composed of the contoso domain name and the .com top-level domain or TLD). URL paths refer to
4 portion of the URL that follows the domain name, starting with a /. The URL https://contoso.com/api
5 has a path of /api.
