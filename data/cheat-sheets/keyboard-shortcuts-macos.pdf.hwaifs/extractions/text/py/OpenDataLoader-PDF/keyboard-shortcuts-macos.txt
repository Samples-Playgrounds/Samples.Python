Navigation





⌘T Show all Symbols ⌃G Go to Line... ⌘P Go to File... ⇧⌘O Go to Symbol... ⇧⌘M Show Problems panel F8 / ⇧F8 Go to next/previous error or warning ⌃⇧Tab Navigate editor group history ⌃- / ⌃⇧- Go back/forward ⌃⇧M Toggle Tab moves focus























Editor management
