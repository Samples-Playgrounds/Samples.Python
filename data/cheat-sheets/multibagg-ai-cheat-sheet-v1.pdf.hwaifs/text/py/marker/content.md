#### I want to find hidden gems

- Use Discovery to explore AI-tagged companies. (Example: Capacity Expansion, Cloud & Data Centers)
- Use Screener to filter companies using fundamentals. (Example filters: ROCE > 18%, Sales Growth > 15%, Debt/Equity < 0.5)
- Use Deals to track smart-money activity. (Example: Promoter buying, Bulk deals, Block deals, Insider trades)
- Ask Iris to discover stocks instantly. (Example: "Find hidden gems in chemicals under 5,000 Cr market cap")

# I want to research a company

- Open the Company Page to see everything in one place
  (Example: Revenue trend, profit trend, margins, balance sheet, peer comparison)
- Switch to AI Mode and ask deep questions
  (Example: "Explain the business model like Im 15" / "What are the top 5 risks?")
- Read Concalls, Presentations, Annual Reports (AI-ready)
  (Example: "Summarize this concall" / "Give key management commentary")
- Use Articles to understand quickly (without noise)
